My Career Journey

Transitioning from IT infrastructure to full-stack development

My career began in IT infrastructure and systems administration, where I developed strong technical foundations and problem-solving skills. Over the years, I've worked on mission-critical systems, led technical teams, and implemented solutions for high-profile events including the World Cup 2014 and the Rio 2016 Olympic Games.

As technology evolved, I found myself increasingly drawn to software development. I began exploring backend development with Python and Django, which eventually led me to discover the power and flexibility of the MERN stack. The ability to build complete, modern applications using JavaScript throughout the stack resonated with my desire to create end-to-end solutions.

Currently, I'm completing Ironhack's intensive web development bootcamp, focusing on MongoDB, Express, React, and Node.js. This formal training complements my years of IT experience, allowing me to bridge the gap between infrastructure and application development.

My unique background gives me a holistic understanding of the development lifecycle, from writing code to deployment and maintenance. I bring a perspective that combines developer creativity with operational stability—a valuable combination in today's technology landscape.
